# LOCALIZE_IT — ELI5 (Explain Like I'm 5)

## The Problem

You use ChatGPT, Claude, or other AI helpers online. They're smart, but:
- They cost money every month
- They watch what you type
- They can disappear or change
- You don't own anything you taught them

## The Solution

**LOCALIZE_IT** = Make your own AI that learns from YOU

## How It Works (3 Steps)

### Step 1: USE Normal AI
Keep using ChatGPT/Claude for hard questions.

### Step 2: CAPTURE Automatically
LOCALIZE_IT secretly saves:
- What you asked
- How you asked it
- What you liked
- How you write

### Step 3: TRAIN Your Own
At night, your computer trains a small AI using YOUR patterns.

## What You Get

- ✅ An AI that talks like you
- ✅ Works without internet
- ✅ Costs $0 after setup
- ✅ You own it completely
- ✅ Runs on your laptop

## The 3 Capture Modes

| Mode | What It Does | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Shadow** | Watches everything | Saves all your questions |
| **Intraday** | Asks questions | "You seem to like bullet points?" |
| **Explicit** | You tell it | "Remember I like Python first" |

## Analogy

> It's like having a twin who watches you work, learns how you think, then becomes your assistant who never needs the internet and never charges you.

## Simple Commands

```bash
localize_it setup          # One-time setup
localize_it shadow-start   # Begin watching
localize_it train          # Create your AI
localize_it chat           # Talk to YOUR AI
```

## Bottom Line

**Cloud AI = Rented brain that watches you**

**LOCALIZE_IT = Your own brain that you own**

Train your own. Own your own. Localize it. 🌿
